FAQ

Still Have Questions?

Do I need any equipment?

Very little. Most people start with their bodyweight and a set of resistance bands, and your coach scales every exercise to whatever you have access to.

I'm an older athlete — can I still see results?

Yes. We work with people well into their 50s and beyond. The system meets you where you are and progresses at a pace your body can handle.

I'm not trying to dunk — I just want to move without pain. Is this still for me?

Absolutely. The goal is a knee you trust, whether that means returning to sport or just hiking, lifting, and living without hesitation.

I can only train a few times a week. Is that a problem?

No. Your plan is built around your schedule. Consistency over time matters far more than how many days a week you can train.

I've already finished physical therapy. How is this different?

Physical therapy gets you cleared. KneeHab bridges the gap between being cleared and being prepared for real-world performance and the activities you love.

Is coaching online? I live outside the US.

Yes — coaching is fully online, so you can work with us from anywhere in the world.

Is this a substitute for medical care?

No. KneeHab is coaching and education, not a substitute for individualized medical care. Always follow the guidance of your own healthcare provider.
