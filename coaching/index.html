FAQ

Questions, Answered

Do I need to be an athlete to work with a coach?

No. We coach athletes, active adults, people recovering from surgery, and people managing chronic knee pain. Every roadmap is built around your starting point and your goals.

Is coaching done online or in person?

Coaching is delivered online, so you can work with us from anywhere. You get a dedicated coach, video form reviews, progress tracking, direct messaging, and regular coaching calls.

I've already finished physical therapy. Is this still for me?

Yes. Most people come to us after they've been cleared but still don't trust their knee. Coaching bridges the gap between being cleared and being prepared for real life and sport.
