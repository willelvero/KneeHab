FAQ

Still Have Questions?

What kinds of injuries do you work with?

Everything — ACL, MCL, LCL, meniscus, patellar tendonitis, runner's knee, post-surgical stiffness, chronic instability, even arthritis and "bone-on-bone." And because the body works as one connected chain, we also train other related issues like ankle or back issues.

How is this different from physical therapy?

We are not against PT, but the difference is the goal. PT gets you "cleared" for daily life, then ends. We take you past that — stronger, more confident, and more athletic than before the injury — by fixing the root cause, not the symptom: we bulletproof the whole chain (feet, ankles, hips, core), not just the knee.

Can I do this alongside PT?

Absolutely — many clients do BOTH, and we build around your PT schedule so the two work together instead of competing for your time.

I've already been cleared by PT. Can I still do this?

Perfect timing. PT usually wraps once you're "cleared" for daily life — and that's exactly where most people get stuck: the knee works, but it isn't strong, confident, or athletic again. Closing that gap is what we're built for.

I'm not a pro athlete — I just want to live without pain.

This is absolutely still for you. Most of our clients aren't chasing a podium — they just want to walk downstairs, hike, keep up with their kids, or play weekend pickup without babying the knee. The training scales to your goals, whatever they are.

Do I need any equipment?

Nope! We actually ship you our resistance bands. Your program will be written with whatever you have access to. If you want to workout from home, we will make that happen. If you have access to a gym we will make sure our plan includes exercises you can do at your gym. Either or, we will make sure your circumstances never hold you back.

Do I need surgery? Is it too late if my injury was years ago?

Neither's a dealbreaker. We work with people at every stage — prehab, fresh post-op, years out, and plenty who NEVER had surgery. And it's never too late: many of our clients have been dealing with knee pain for 5, 10, even 20+ years before we worked together. An old injury just means the surrounding muscles have been compensating a long time — exactly what we fix.

How does the coaching work?

You get a coach and a fully customized plan in an app. You log your workouts, your coach tracks the data to find your weak links, and you hop on Zoom calls as often as you need to adjust. You also get a full education library and weekly group Q&A calls.

Can I incorporate upper body or my own training?

Absolutely. Your plan is built around your goals, equipment, and schedule — and that can include full upper-body and sport-specific work. Some clients will send us their splits and we'll build around it, others have us program everything for them head to toe.

I'm really busy — how much time does this take?

As little or as much as you want. We cycle high and low days — bigger sessions run 45–70 minutes, short ones are a 10–20 minute workout you can knock out before bed. Busy on a random Tuesday or going on vacation? No problem. Just talk with your coach, we will fix your schedule to make it work. At the end of the day, consistency beats intensity every time.

How soon will I see results?

Many people feel a difference in the first few weeks — usually from finally training consistently and training the whole chain, not just the knee. Lasting life-long results typically come over 3–6 months. We won't promise a date, but it compounds fast.
